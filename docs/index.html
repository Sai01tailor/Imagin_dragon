(1)Dan Reynolds

Daniel Coulter Reynolds (born July 14, 1987) is an American singer, songwriter, and musician. He is the lead vocalist and a founding member of the pop rock band Imagine Dragons, which formed in 2008. He is a recipient of the Songwriters Hall of Fame Hal David Starlight Award.

(2) Wayne Sermon

Wayne Sermon is the lead guitarist of the American rock band Imagine Dragons. Known for his dynamic guitar work and atmospheric soundscapes, he plays a key role in shaping the band’s unique alt-rock style. A classically trained musician from Berklee College of Music, Sermon blends technical precision with emotional depth. His stage presence and versatile playing have contributed to the band’s global success, making him an influential figure in modern rock and a defining force behind Imagine Dragons' sound.

(3) Ben McKee

Ben McKee is the bassist of the Grammy-winning band Imagine Dragons. Known for his dynamic stage presence and musical versatility, he joined the band in 2009. A core contributor to the group’s signature sound, Ben blends rock, pop, and electronic elements seamlessly. Beyond music, he’s also recognized for his philanthropy and deep connection with fans.
